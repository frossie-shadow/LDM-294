\subsubsection*{1.02C.05.00: Management, Leadership, \& Other LOE}

This WBS element covers Project Management, Control Account Management, and Scientific Leadership of the Science User Interface and Tools group. It includes effort to develop the overall schedule and cost for the work, perform day-to-day technical management of the team, coordinate with other parts of the WBS, provide support for invoices, contribute to the operation of the DM Subsystem Science Team, and LOE (for meetings ...) for team members.
