\subsubsection*{1.02C.06.00: Management, Leadership, and Other LOE}

This WBS element covers Project Management, Control Account Management, and technical leadership of the Science Data Archive and Data Access Services group. It includes effort to develop the overall schedule and cost for the work, perform day-to-day technical management of the team, coordinate with other parts of the WBS, provide support for invoices, and LOE (meetings, etc.) for team members.
